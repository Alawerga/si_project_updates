The group project started with the formation of Vibe Coders. We divided the workload into requirements design and planning modules where I took charge of the class diagrams and other members handled the database and use cases using tools like StarUML and Excalidraw, I used gaphor.  The fisrt lab was complicated, the teacher was very tough during the lab session calling out parts of our documentation that lacked technical depth and pointing out that the decisions regarding the MVVM pattern (which was my part) and the separation of logic from the GUI components (again my fault, I did everything wrong) were a major problem. We spent the following 2 days in a crunch to restructure the entire project where I redesingned the entire UML diagram and adding features and expanding our GitHub issues to a total of 151 tasks. After a long weekend of crunch we successfully submitted the revised materials and received a final grade of 8.6. There was a group discussion on how to fairly distribute the integer grades among the eight of us that took a few days to settle. Now we will be moving into the implementation.
